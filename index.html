👋 Biography

I am a tenure-track Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science at Knox College. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering in 2025 and my B.S. in Computer Science at The University of Alabama. My advisor was Dr. Fei Hu from The University of Alabama and my co-advisor is Dr. Yu Gan from Stevens Institute of Technology.

My research lies at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Extended Reality (XR).
Currently working on:

  1. Video anomaly reasoning with vision-language models
  2. Neural rendering on XR devices
