Biography

I am a PhD student in Electrical Computer Engineering at the University of Alabama. I got my Bachelor’s Degrees in Computer Science from The University of Alabama

My reserach interests is Computer Vision, Deep Learning and Mixed Reality. My mission is to make machine understand visual scence and convey realistic visual information to assist human.

Debug the world! is my slogan and reflect my perspective of the world. Our society need to be a better place than before by trying to find “bugs” in life and fix it.

I make music, AMVs and tutorials. My skills are not mature but I am keep increase my skills. I have an YouTube channel and a Bilibili channel.

I was vice president of The Association of Chinese Students and Scholars.
